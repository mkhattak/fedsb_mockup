Capabilities / Index

1. Identity Management

What is it?

Identity Management is the service where accounts are created, modified, and deleted.

2. Credential Management

What is it?

Credential Management is the service where credentials such passwords, certificates are managed.

3. Directory / Storage

What is it?

Directory / Storage is the management of identities and attributes in a directory or database.

4. Authentication

What is it?

Authentication is the service where we determine who is allowed on the network and who is not.

5. Authorization

What is it?

Authorization is the service where we determine what a person is allowed to access.
